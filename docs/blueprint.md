# **App Name**: Meat Farmstead

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Visually appealing hero section with high-quality images of fresh meat, a compelling headline, and clear CTAs for 'Select Meat' and 'Request a Call'.
- Product Catalog: Detailed product catalog with categorized sections for beef, pork, lamb, whole carcasses, and prepared products, each with images, descriptions, and pricing.
- Online Ordering System: Shopping cart functionality allowing customers to select products, specify quantities, and proceed to checkout with various payment options.
- Delivery and Payment Options: Clearly outlined delivery zones, conditions (minimum order, free delivery threshold), and accepted payment methods (cash, card, online).
- AI-Powered Product Description Enhancement: Use an AI tool to enhance the quality and SEO friendliness of the descriptions in the product catalog.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rustic brown (#A0522D) to convey a sense of warmth, tradition, and earthiness related to farming and natural products.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a clean, neutral backdrop that enhances the visibility of the meat products.
- Accent color: Burgundy (#800020) to highlight calls to action and important information, reflecting the rich color of meat and adding a touch of elegance.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif with a high-end feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' a modern but accessible and readable san-serif font, to complement the 'Playfair' headline font. 
- Simple, rustic icons to represent product categories and key benefits (e.g., no hormones, fresh delivery).
- Clean, minimalist design with ample white space to emphasize the quality of the product images.
